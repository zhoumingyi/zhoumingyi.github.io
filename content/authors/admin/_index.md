I am currently a third-year PhD student at Monash University in Australia, where I have the privilege of being mentored by [Prof John Grundy](https://sites.google.com/site/johncgrundy/), [Prof Li Li](https://lilicoding.github.io/), [Prof Chunyang Chen](https://chunyang-chen.github.io/), and [Dr Xiao Chen](https://research.monash.edu/en/persons/xiao-chen). I was a member of Monash SMAT Lab, leading by Prof Li Li. My current research project is focused on using program analysis to enhance the reliability of deployed machine learning (ML) systems, especially for smart mobile apps. This includes assessing the risks associated with ML systems, protecting their intellectual property, and minimizing their attacking surface. Additionally, I am exploring ways to optimize machine learning programs post-deployment to improve inference efficiency.
